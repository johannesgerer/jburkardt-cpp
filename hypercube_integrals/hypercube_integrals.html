HYPERCUBE_INTEGRALS
Integrals Over the Interior of the Unit Hypercube in M Dimensions

HYPERCUBE_INTEGRALS is a C++ library which returns the exact value of the integral of any monomial over the interior of the unit hypercube in M dimensions.

The interior of the unit hypercube in M dimensions is defined by 

        0 <= X(1:M) <= 1.

The integrands are all of the form 

        F(X) = product ( 1 <= I <= M ) X(I)^E(I)
where the exponents are nonnegative integers.
